In figure 2, the user can compare total number of employed persons against unemployed in Finland, between 2007 to 2022. Through the buttons the user can also compare the number of employed persons against total population in Finland between 2007 to 2022. The Employment figure represents employed persons for all industries and all sex, whereas unemployment figure represents number of jobseekers from January to December, for all industries, all sex and all education level.
